FOUNDER OF TEMITOPE AFRICAN DESIGN

My Name is Temitope Oluwatuyi born and raised in Nigeria. A full time college student at Queensborough Community College Major in Information Technology. I hoped people could see the best in building my AFRICAN clothing website because i am proud of my culture attires

Temitope (TE-MI-TO-PE) is a ready-to-wear bold print clothing line offering quality, trendy African inspired fashion at affordable prices. With his love for fashion and passion for self expression through unique clothing, Nigerian born Temitope Oluwatuyi launched the TEMITOPE brand at the beginning of 2015. TEMITOPE is a fun, bold, chic line centered on inspiring individuals to confidently dress bold and beautifully. All garments are currently made in Philadelphia with great attention to quality.

Our Brand


Temitope & Afican, Inc. is a leading specialty manufacturer and retailer of women's fashion apparel and accessories providing women with modern, wear to work solutions that are multi-functional at affordable prices. The Company was incorporated in 2013 and has been public since 2015. It is headquartered in New York City.

New Temitope & african's proprietary branded New York & Company merchandise is sold exclusively through its national network of over 500 New York & Company retail stores, New York & Company outlet stores, and E-commerce store at Temmydesign.com.

New York & Company exists to make women look great and feel good. We make our customers feel confident, put-together, attractive, and fashion-right.

purchasing a gift card:

you can add an electronic gift card to your cart from the main gift certificate page. once the gift card has been added to your cart, you can continue shopping, or checkout.

upon placing your order, the electronic gift card(s) will be emailed to the recipients. the recipient will receive an email alerting them that they have received a gift card, and provide them with a gift code.

please note that gift cards purchased on OAKNYC.COM can only be used to purchase items online at OAKNYC.COM, and gift cards purcased in one of our four NY and LA brick and mortar stores can only be used to purchased items in an OAK brick and mortar store.

redeeming a gift code:

customers can use their gift code to purchase items from oaknyc.com. during the checkout process customers have the option of using a gift code for payment. in the event that the gift code’s value does not cover the total cost of the order, the customer will need to provide their credit card information to cover the remaining balance. in the event that the order total is less than the value of the gift code, the remaining gift code balance will be available for future purchases.

please note that currently gift certificates purchased online are virtual and not redeemable at our brick and mortar locations. we apologize for the inconvenience. to purchase a physical gift certificate please contact one of our retail locations directly.
